Customer Data Policy

Prime Shoe Jersey Hub acts as the controller of the personal data you share with us through this website, checkout flow, and support channels. We value customer privacy and handle information with reasonable care, confidentiality, and security.

Personal Information We Collect

When you visit the website, we may automatically collect limited technical information such as your device type, browser, IP address, time zone, cookies, and pages viewed. This helps us understand site usage and improve performance.

When you place an order or contact us, we may collect the information you provide directly, including your name, phone number, delivery address, email address, and any details shared in support messages.

Why We Process Your Data

We process customer data only as needed to operate the website, fulfill orders, verify payments, arrange shipping, respond to support requests, and protect the store from fraud or misuse.

Without certain personal details, we may not be able to confirm your order, arrange delivery, or provide customer support.

Payment Screenshots and WhatsApp Communication

Because our checkout process uses manual UPI verification, customers may need to share a payment screenshot on WhatsApp or another support channel. These screenshots may contain limited transaction details and are used only for payment confirmation, order validation, issue resolution, and internal record keeping.

WhatsApp conversations may also be used to confirm order status, share tracking information, clarify address details, or resolve problems related to delivery and exchanges.

Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights related to the personal information we hold about you, including the right to access, correct, request deletion of, or object to certain uses of your data.

Links to Other Websites

Our website may include links to third-party websites or social platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites, and we recommend reviewing their privacy policies separately.

Information Security

We maintain reasonable administrative, technical, and operational safeguards to protect customer information from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. However, no online method of transmission or storage is completely risk-free.

Legal Disclosure

We may disclose information when required by law, when responding to lawful requests, when investigating fraud, or when necessary to protect the rights, safety, and operations of Prime Shoe Jersey Hub or its customers.
