Shipping and Dispatch

We aim to keep delivery timelines clear and reliable. Every order is manually verified before it moves to packing and dispatch, which helps reduce errors and improve delivery accuracy.

Order Processing Time

Orders are reviewed and prepared after payment verification. In most cases, verified orders are processed and dispatched within 1 to 3 working days from the time payment is confirmed.

Dispatch After Payment Verification

Dispatch begins only after your UPI payment has been verified by our team. If payment confirmation or screenshot clarification is pending, dispatch may be delayed until verification is complete.

Delivery Timeline

Typical delivery timelines range from 2 to 7 working days after dispatch, depending on your location and courier routing. Metro cities may receive orders faster, while remote or high-load regions may take longer.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you may receive tracking details through WhatsApp, email, or another support channel. You can use these details to monitor your shipment status.

No Cash on Delivery

We do not offer Cash on Delivery for this checkout flow. Orders are accepted through manual UPI payment only and are confirmed after verification.

Incorrect Address or Contact Details

Please ensure that your shipping address, phone number, and other delivery details are correct when placing your order. We are not responsible for failed deliveries caused by incomplete or incorrect information submitted at checkout.
