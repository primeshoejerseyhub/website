Prime Shoe Jersey Hub Policy

Our refund and exchange process is designed to stay fair, transparent, and easy to understand. Because every order is manually reviewed after UPI payment, approved resolutions are handled only after the original payment has been verified.

No Refunds or Returns

We do not offer routine refunds or returns once an order has been placed and successfully delivered. Please review product details, sizing, and delivery information carefully before confirming your purchase.

Exchange Eligibility

Exchange requests are accepted only when there is a genuine issue with the order. An exchange may be considered if:

  • The product was damaged during transit.
  • The product has a manufacturing defect.
  • You received a different style or size from what was ordered.

Important: exchanges are not approved when the delivered item matches the style and size selected by the customer at checkout. Please review the size chart before placing your order.

To qualify for an exchange, the item must be unused, unwashed, in original condition, and returned with tags and original packaging intact.

Exchange requests must be submitted within 48 hours of delivery. Requests raised after this period may not be accepted.

Refund Policy

Refunds are only considered in limited situations, such as when a verified order cannot be fulfilled or when an approved issue cannot be resolved through replacement or exchange.

Refunds are only processed after payment verification. If a refund is approved, it will be initiated to the original payment source or arranged through a verified manual method within a reasonable processing period.

How to Request an Exchange

To request an exchange, please contact our support team on WhatsApp and share the following:

  1. Your Order ID.
  2. Clear photos of the received product and the issue.
  3. A short explanation of the problem.
  4. Your payment screenshot if requested for verification.

Once your request is received, our team will review the information and guide you through the next steps for a smooth resolution.
